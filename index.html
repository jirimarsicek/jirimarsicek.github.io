About

I have started my career in 1977 as a magazine photographer for Motoristická současnost (later renamed to Motor). After its publishing ended in 1990 I worked as a freelance photographer with leading automotive magazines in Czechia including Svět motorů, Autotip and Automobil Revue.

Motorsport

Large portion of my work was dedicated to various motorsport events. I have a vast collection of pictures documenting both Czech and Europian motorsport events throughout the history.

Life

I have met many exceptional people and captured their life and surroundings through a lens of my camera.
